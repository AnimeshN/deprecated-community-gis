SWM Maps

This is a Community based platform . Objective is to evolve it into a tool for community action which any local community can undertake in their locality. To start with it will help visualising the state of waste management. The Census data, buildings data from voter lists and DP layers , schools layer etc will help contextualise the waste management in a given area . To encourage best practices including waste seggregation, disposal etc a composite index of greenness will be evolved. We shall take a cue from the weightages PCMC has used to incentivise property tax. Important change will be to introduce weightage for menstrual waste seggregation, and disposal.It is expected that trends toward reduction of ( wet and dry and menstrual ) waste per capita will help community to spur action.

Education Maps

This ( GIS + CMS i.e geographical information system and content management system) is proposed to be community platform to represent educational informtion including location. This will help evaluate and project access, quantity and quality of education from pre-primary to professional education. Community organisations should be able to take up this exercise for monitoring their own area of interest, granularly. As of now there appears to be no reliable map enabled resource for visualising location of educational institions even though location (as in distance from residence) is crucial factor in laws relating to Right To Education ( RTE).This platform should also help analysing adequacy of amenities when combined with demography and other census data which are also in maps. Assesement of achievements of learning objectives could also figure in, in this platform. It is proposed that educational NGOs own up this page and use the same to assert the rights of the underprivileged as also to improve collaborations among institutions across the levels.

Transportation Maps

Bus transportation is a carrier of development and an indicator of human development. The following shapefiles combine census data, bus depot operational data and GIS data of Shahapur taluka. The data tries to show routes that are profitable / lossy , number of bus services passing through any point of importance in taluka etc. This data not only helps in decision support but also holistic access of transportation to the community, thereby fostering development.
Data sets used:
MRSAC:road network of Thane district
 MSRTC:Operational data of Shahapur Taluka, Jan-Mar 2019
